The Crew

Meet the officers, sailors, and key historical figures aboard the HMS Gloucester.

Main Crew Members

Captain Henry Aubrey Rowley

Commanding officer of the HMS Gloucester during its fateful voyage.

Lieutenant Commander R. P. Tanner, RN

Served from 1940-1941 and was second in command.

Master Gunner Richard Leake

Alerted others to the ship's impending doom when it ran aground on sandbanks.

Petty Officer Harold Raymond Allen

Supervises junior personnel and acted as a technical specialist.

Able Seaman Albert Allsop

Skilled deck crew member who performed a wide range of duties, including steering the sihp, maintaining the vessel etc.

Chief Engineer G Thomas

The senior technical leader reponsible for overseeing all engineering projects, ensureing they are completed on time, on budget and to a high standard
